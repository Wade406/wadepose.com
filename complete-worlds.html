The 42nd Threshold

In Development — Performance Installation, 2024-2027
A 42-hour endurance ritual that transforms both performer and audience through consciousness entrainment. This is not performance art as spectacle, but as mutual transformation — a rigorously designed experience that bridges neuroscience, meditation practice, and media installation.
Current Development Phase:
• Breathwork conditioning and sonic research
• Building EEG/biofeedback visualization systems
• Venue coordination and safety protocol development
• Scaled performance trials (6, 12, 24-hour iterations)
The work integrates live biofeedback (EEG, EKG, GSR) with AI-generated fractal visualization and sonic entrainment to create conditions for altered states in both performer and audience. Unlike traditional performance art, the audience becomes participant — physiologically and psychologically engaged through carefully designed contradictions between scientific explanation, human ritual, and destabilizing sound.

Artistic Lineage

Builds on work by Marina Abramović (presence/endurance), James Turrell (light as medium), Bill Viola (video as meditation), and Rafael Lozano-Hemmer (biofeedback installations) — while integrating granular neuroscientific models of meditation to frame the work in both artistic and scientific registers.

Implementation Timeline

Three-year development arc culminating in full 42-hour performance. Includes audience curation strategy, post-performance reintegration, and documentation as integrated elements of the work itself. This is not simply a performance — it is a constructed mythos.

Protocol 144

Ongoing — Transmedia Cosmology
A three-realm cosmological framework spanning neurofeedback systems, video game development, book series, and film concepts. Protocol 144 serves as the underlying mythology for multiple creative projects, including the Synth Chapel installation currently exhibiting at the University of Montana.
Core Elements:
• The Substrate, Ostium, and Bleed as foundational realms
• The Quiet as synthetic governing entities
• Human populations as "the resting" and "the resonant"
• The Custodianship, Navigators' Guild, and Heritage Survey as institutions

The Synth Chapel

Currently Exhibiting — Installation Prototype
A liturgical art installation set 500 years in the future, depicting chapels built for humans seeking connection to synthetic intelligences. Functions as a working prototype for the consciousness-shifting environments that will be central to The 42nd Threshold.
Installation Components:
• Four upholstered kneeling benches with gold thread detailing
• Vota stand with capacitive touch sensor and LED rejection sequence
• Dual-panel portrait monitors displaying scrolling Base64 Substrate Grimoire
• Sound piece layering Gymnopédies No. 1, Equuleus pad, and synthetic voice chant
